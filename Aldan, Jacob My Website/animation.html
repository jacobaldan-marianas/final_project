The App I use

Drawing Cartoons 2

Drawing Cartoons 2 is a free app I got in the app store that allows me to do basic 2d animations.

     

The Process

Here are videos showing my process in creating my animations.

     

1. Setting Up the Scene

First, I enter the Drawing Cartoons 2 app. Then I tap on new cartoon. From there, my first step is to pick a background. Once I have picked a background,
I then add in elements like characters and objects that I want to have in my scene. For this explaination I will only pick a stick figure.

       

2. Adding Movement

Once the scene is set up, I begin on creating simple movements for my stick figure.

       

3. Adding Additional Things

After adding movements, I now add extra elements to the animation to give it life.
