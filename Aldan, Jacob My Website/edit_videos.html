The App I use

Kinemaster

Kinemaster is a free app I got in the App Store that allows me to edit videos I have in my phone gallery.

     

The Process

Here are videos showing my process in editing my videos.

     

1. Create New

Here I tap on the button "Create New" to make a blank editor for me to start (don't mind the ads, it is a free app afterall).

       

2. Setting Up the Scene/How the Unedited Footage Looks Like

Here I pick 2 videos I took from my phone camera and insert them into the blank editor.

       

3. Cutting the Videos

Once I have my videos inserted, I start by cutting out the unnecassary parts of the videos.

       

4. Putting the Videos Together

After cutting the videos, I then combine them together into approriate parts.

       

5. Adding Filter

Once I am done combining the videos together, I now add a filter to the appropriate parts.

       

6. Adding Music

I now add music to the appropriate parts.

       

Exporting It to My Phone Gallery

Once I am done editing, I export the finished product to my phone gallery to upload to Youtube.
